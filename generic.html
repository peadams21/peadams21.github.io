Deer Crossing

Guide a mighty buck across a busy Yooper highway. Use your arrow keys or touch screen to dodge traffic. Make it to the other side safely, and you'll earn a golden pasty.

StarCat

Blast the sad cats with love to make them happy! Use your arrow keys or touch screen to move the spaceship and space bar or screen tap to shoot hearts. Collect yarn balls for bonus points! You'll lose 10 points if sad cats reach the bottom. Every 1000 points, face off against the mighty Grumpy Cat boss who shoots hairballs - avoid them and hit the boss with hearts to defeat it!
